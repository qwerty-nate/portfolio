THE
BACKGROUND

2005
Computer Engineering
Hardware · Assembly · Manufacturing
Microsoft Apple HP Android

COMPUTING FOUNDATIONS

In 2005, I joined a computing class led by Bill Gates and Steve Wozniak — part of a United Nations and British Commonwealth initiative to advance education in the developing world. I was trained on the hardware of the era, translation from assembly to machine code, and the electrical basis of programming.

In 2010, a push for North American manufacturing led to my employment with Hewlett-Packard. I became a metallurgist and manufacturing technician, assembling laptops and personal computers from the component level up.

In 2012 I began working for the Open Handset Alliance — the consortium behind Android. After training in manufacturing and mobile programming, I led a team dedicated to building an all-metal premium device to compete with the iPhone and Galaxy lines. My team created the HTC One series and the Sense interface layer for Android. These years culminated in a Master's in Engineering from Stanford and a PhD from the University of the West Indies, with theses spanning battery stability, electrical conductivity, network reliability, CPU/GPU dual processing, and dynamic memory management for mobile and IoT.

2009
Software Development
Social Media · Gaming · VR
META YEEZY HTC Niantic

SOFTWARE, GAMES & VIRTUAL REALITY

I began working for Facebook in 2009 after my posts and memes became explosively popular on the internet. The company wanted to extend the platform beyond text and images and get involved in gaming. I trained in HTML, CSS, JS, Adobe Flash, OpenGL, and MySQL, building games including FarmVille and Ninja Saga.

I wrote and reviewed code on message integrity, self-deleting files, and emoji integration for the major emerging social platforms of that generation — Instagram, Snapchat, WhatsApp, Douyin, and Line. I then turned focus to mobile gaming, creating Android emulators for the PSP and Game Boy Advance, with ports of Pokémon and Street Fighter.

My group was approached by Kardashian-West to join the building of Kim Kardashian: Hollywood — an opportunity that evolved into an in-house gaming studio at YEEZY and investments into eSports, directly leading to the creation of virtual reality hardware.

The historical business partnership between Trinidad & Tobago, Korea, and Japan — and the friendship between Kanye West, Hideo Kojima, and Takashi Murakami — resulted in the creation of the HTC VIVE. I worked on concept, development, and manufacturing management on the factory floors.

2013
Materials Science
R&D · Simulation · Fashion
YEEZY ADIDAS PETROTRIN SKIMS

MATERIALS SCIENCE & YEEZY

In 2013, I approached Kanye West with a proposal for PETROTRIN to become the materials supplier for YEEZY's partnership with ADIDAS — refining crude oil into nylon, acrylic, polyester fabrics, and polyurethane soles. I was hired into the consortium and trained on full-cycle material development.

Material R&D was being done by pen, paper, and iterative lab testing. Team discussions led us to build material modeling software specifically for the fashion industry — based on COMSOL Multiphysics and Unreal Engine, these tools modeled chemical and physical material interactions and simulated human use of clothing in real-time.

The creation of this software required recording chemical syntheses and fashion shows, then describing the data to our machines. Our work produced major insights into GPU-based computing and natural language processing that would reverberate far beyond fashion.

HUJI & NETWORKED COMPUTER VISION

HUJI began as a automated photoenhancement app, applying filters to give images the look of a film photo from the 1980s. The artificial intelligence was improved to be able to identify and edit facial features, posture etc. Beyond photomanipulation, HUJI was able to embed and derive large amounts of information from pictures.

In 2021 I used HUJI AI to break blockchain encryption, mapping hashes to a color representation then brute forcing with high ends GPUs. Following this, I was approached by Elon Musk to help with networked computer vision for automated vehicles.

I rewrote the logic of computing sequential visual information and improved the efficiency of communicating visuospatial data between the Tesla network and the city surveillance grid.

DIPLOMACY & MILITARY SERVICE

A dual citizen of the United States and Trinidad & Tobago, I have served in the militaries of both nations as an intelligence agent, trained in self-defense and political action. My ethics are humanist and futurist with an acute awareness of ecological impact.

I served as an Ambassador of Trinidad and Tobago, representing my country's energy and technology sectors in international conferences and legal proceedings. I have championed the water and energy rights of Indigenous Peoples and the advancement of solar energy and biofuels — as a means to balance the ecological impact of the technologies I have built.
